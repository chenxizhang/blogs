# Office 2007中的简繁体转换功能 
> 原文发表于 2008-12-21, 地址: http://www.cnblogs.com/chenxizhang/archive/2008/12/21/1359299.html 


Word 2007默认就有简繁体转换功能

 [![image](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_thumb.png)](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_2.png) 

  

 Excel 2007默认没有开启，可以通过下面的方式打开

 [![image](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_thumb_1.png)](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_4.png) 

 选择“COM加载项”，然后点击“转到”。在下图中确保选中了"Chinese Translation Addin"

 [![image](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_thumb_2.png)](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_6.png) 

 然后，也可以在审阅窗格中看到简繁体转换的工具栏按钮

 [![image](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_thumb_3.png)](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_8.png) 

  

 Powerpoint 2007的使用方式和Excel是一样的

  

 Outlook 2007的设置方法比较特殊。因为Outlook的界面其实没有完全改掉。所以，我们得在新建一个邮件，的那个编辑器窗口中点击“编辑器选项”

 [![image](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_thumb_4.png)](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_10.png) 

 然后，自定义，所有命令，里面可以找到两个有关的命令，将他们添加到快捷访问工具栏即可

 [![image](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_thumb_5.png)](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_12.png) 

 [![image](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_thumb_6.png)](http://www.cnblogs.com/images/cnblogs_com/chenxizhang/WindowsLiveWriter/Office2007_E546/image_14.png)
